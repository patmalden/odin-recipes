Fries

Fries... the perfect match for the perfect food (burger)

Fries are the next best thing compared to burgers.

With this recipe you can have a match made in heaven! Let's get started

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Spices
  • Oil
  1. Chop your potatoes into fry shape
  2. Grease a baking sheet
  3. Place your fries on the sheet and spice them
  4. Brush the fries with oil
  5. Bake in oven until golden brown
